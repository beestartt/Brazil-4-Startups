Brazil4Startups offers a complete market research that will bring a complete vision of the potential and challenges for your business in Brazil.

We work with three aspects of market research: Primary, secondary and exploratory, always taking into account the specificities and characteristics of each business. Our objective is to show with clear data the opportunities and challenges in the Brazilian market for your business segment.

Go-to-market

Go-to-market is the strategy that allows companies and / or products to be disclosed, sold and provide the support needed for a specific customer profile. Among its advantages are revenue growth, profitability and a loyal public. In summary your company should be able to answer with 5 key questions:

Who are you selling to?

What product do you offer this audience?

How will it reach the target audience?

What will be the price and positioning of the product?

What channel will you use to sell?
